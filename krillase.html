What Is Krillase®?

    Treatments

  • Dissolves Thrombogenic Plaque

    Krillase® safely dissolves arterial thrombogenic plaque safely and efficiently

  • Chronic Wounds and Burns

    In thirteen clinical studies comprising 551 patients Krillase® has demonstrated it promotes faster healing and supports existing grafting for chronic wounds and burns.

  • Thrombus

    The enzymes in Krillase® has demonstrated it can completely dissolve fresh and organized thrombus faster and more effectively than currently approved fibrinolytic therapy without having a potential for bleed outs.

    Development

  • What is Krillase®

    A “biochemical knife” that breaks down necrotic tissue and thrombogenic material that can mitigate or treat multiple disease states in humans.

  • Medical Device Pathway

    In certain geographies and for certain conditions Krillase® can be considered a Class III medical device for topical applications.

  • Medical Device Pathway

    Krillase® will initially be delivered as a sterile, lyophilized powder with long shelf life.

How Does Krillase® Work?

  • Active Ingredient: Krill

    In the Antarctic regions, krill represent an enormous biomass and constitute a major food source for higher forms of life.

    Food supplies in the Antarctic region are very limited most of the year requiring powerful enzymes for Krill to survive. Food is only available in abundance in the short summer season when sunlight allows the primary production in the ocean to accelerate. As a result, krill depend on building up energy reserves to survive and possess an unusually effective digestive apparatus and set of enzymes

  • Biochemical Knife

    Antarctic Krill survives in very cold water using specialized enzyme system to digest food, Peptide hydrolase, a mix of proteinases / peptidases.

    This provides a unique “cutting” capability inside and on the end of dental and biofilm proteins.

    Krillase® unique digestion capabilities significantly reduces the adhesion properties of oral microflora and rapidly dissolves complex biochemical substrates.
