Marizyme, Inc., Acquires Protease Drug Platform and Appoints New CEO

  • Sep 27, 2018
  • by admin
  • 2 Comments

publicly traded (OTC:MRZM) company focusing on acquiring late-stage drug assets for use in the acute care space for development and commercialization, announced today that they have acquired their first drug assets. Marizyme completed an Asset Purchase Agreement for a novel protease drug platform that has had extensive clinical testing in Europe. This protease platform is a novel combination of enzymes first isolated and developed by Pharmacia Corporation in Sweden. It has been tested in over 551 patients and has demonstrated safety and efficacy for multiple indications including wound healing, thrombogenic disease and anti-viral applications.
